Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a good ecommerce agency in Sweden?
Platform certification (Shopify Plus or Adobe Commerce), experience with Swedish payments (Klarna, Swish), Swedish ERP knowledge, proven client track record, industry vertical expertise, and transparent project methodology.
What ecommerce services do you offer?
End-to-end: strategy, Shopify/Adobe Commerce development, custom themes, ERP integration, B2B portals, payment/shipping, Core Web Vitals, SEO, Klaviyo email, and ongoing support retainers.
How much does an ecommerce project cost?
Basic Shopify store 30,000–80,000 SEK, custom theme 50,000–150,000 SEK, Shopify Plus 150,000–500,000 SEK, Adobe Commerce 500,000–2,000,000 SEK. Hourly rates: 1,200–2,500 SEK/hour.
Do you work with B2B ecommerce?
Yes. Shopify Plus B2B portals and Adobe Commerce B2B with company accounts, customer pricing, requisition lists, quote management, and PunchOut for large corporate buyers.
How long does a project take?
Basic Shopify store 4–8 weeks, custom theme with integrations 8–16 weeks, Shopify Plus B2B 12–24 weeks, Adobe Commerce 20–52 weeks. We deliver in agile sprints with weekly demos.
How do you approach performance?
We measure technical (LCP under 2.5s, INP under 200ms, CLS under 0.1), commercial (conversion rate, AOV, cart abandonment), and organic (SEO rankings) performance. KPI targets are agreed before launch.
