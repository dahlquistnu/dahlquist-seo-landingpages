Frequently Asked Questions

What does a Shopify Expert do?
A Shopify Expert handles store setup, custom theme development, app integration, platform migration, performance optimization, Shopify Plus implementation, and ongoing maintenance. They are verified by Shopify and listed on the partner directory.
How much does a Shopify Expert in Sweden cost?
Rates range from 1,200 to 2,500 SEK per hour. A basic store setup costs 30,000–80,000 SEK, a custom theme 50,000–150,000 SEK, and a full Shopify Plus implementation 150,000–500,000 SEK. Ongoing retainers start from 5,000–15,000 SEK/month.
What's the difference between a Shopify Expert and Shopify Partner?
All Shopify Experts are Shopify Partners, but not all partners are Experts. Experts are a vetted subset approved by Shopify to list on the Expert Marketplace with verified project history and client reviews. Shopify Plus Partners handle enterprise accounts.
Can you migrate my store from Magento or WooCommerce?
Yes. We migrate products, customer data, order history, URLs (301 redirects), and SEO metadata. Magento migrations take 8–16 weeks, WooCommerce 4–8 weeks. We preserve your SEO rankings through careful URL mapping.
Do you handle Swedish-specific requirements?
Yes. We handle Klarna and Swish payment integration, Swedish VAT configuration, GDPR compliance, Swedish ERP systems (Monitor, Fortnox, Visma), and Swedish shipping carriers (PostNord, DHL, Bring).
What is Shopify Hydrogen and should we use it?
Hydrogen is Shopify's React framework for headless storefronts — maximum design freedom and performance but significantly more development cost. Ideal for brands with unique UX requirements and high traffic (500k+ monthly visitors). For most merchants, an optimized Shopify theme delivers excellent results at a fraction of the cost.
